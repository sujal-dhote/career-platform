# NRsolution4u Assistant - Example Usage

## How to Use the Enhanced NRsolution4u Assistant

The NRsolution4u Assistant now features **conversation context awareness** - it remembers your previous messages and provides relevant, personalized responses based on your chat history.

## 🧠 **Conversation Context Feature - All Agents**

### **How It Works:**
- **All 4 agents** remember your last 10 messages
- Each agent understands context relevant to their specialty
- Provides progressive information without repetition
- Builds on previous topics naturally across all agent types

### **Agent-Specific Context Examples:**

#### **NRsolution4u Assistant:**
**User**: "I'm studying computer science"
**Assistant**: [Provides CS-relevant information]

**User**: "What internship programs do you have?"
**Assistant**: "Perfect! Since you're studying computer science, NRsolution4u's internship program would be ideal for you..."

#### **Internship Agent:**
**User**: "I'm a marketing student"
**Assistant**: [Provides marketing internship guidance]

**User**: "What companies hire marketing interns?"
**Assistant**: "Based on your marketing background, here are companies that actively recruit marketing students..."

#### **Job Agent:**
**User**: "I have 3 years of experience in sales"
**Assistant**: [Provides mid-level sales job guidance]

**User**: "I want to transition to tech sales"
**Assistant**: "Given your 3 years of sales experience, transitioning to tech sales is a great move. Your existing sales skills will transfer well..."

#### **Career Consultant:**
**User**: "I want to become a team leader"
**Assistant**: [Provides leadership development advice]

**User**: "What skills should I focus on?"
**Assistant**: "Considering your goal to become a team leader, let me recommend specific leadership skills that will help you advance..."

## 🎯 **Context-Aware Benefits**

### **1. No Repetition**
- Won't repeat basic information already covered
- Builds progressively on previous topics
- Saves time in conversations

### **2. Personalized Responses**
- Tailors recommendations to your stated interests
- Adjusts complexity based on your experience level
- Connects previous topics to current questions

### **3. Natural Flow**
- References previous discussions: "As you mentioned earlier..."
- Continues topics: "Following up on your interest in..."
- Maintains conversation thread

### **4. Smart Recommendations**
- Links your field of study to relevant programs
- Suggests next steps based on conversation flow
- Provides contextually relevant information
